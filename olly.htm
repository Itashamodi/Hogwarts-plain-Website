Olly

Olly is the teacher of Mathematics at Hogwarts School if Witchcraft and Wizardry. Known for her simpler ways of solving complex problems, Olly has published 1500 papers in international and national journals. She teaches with a lot of enthusiasm. Her favourite student is Tux Torvalds. Olly is gentle and has a tender heart. She has been teaching in the institute since 14 years and is a proud acheiver of the Innovating Minds award. Her areas of interest include Probability, Combinatorics and Algebra.
