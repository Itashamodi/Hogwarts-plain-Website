Rosa Ellis Morgan

Rosa Ellis Morgan is a teacher of Muggle Studies in Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Known for her generous nature towards students and her genius, Rosa is a gem of a teacher. She began her career in this school at the age of 22 and has been teaching ever since. With a teaching experience of 7 years, Rosa is the youngest teacher in the school. She has a track record of acheiving excellent results in OWL Examinations.
