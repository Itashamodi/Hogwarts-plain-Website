HufflePuff House

Hufflepuff house was found by Helga Hufflepuff. The house's colors are yellow and black. The emblematic animal is a badger. Typical traits of a Hufflepuff include dedication, hardwork, fairplay, patience, kindness, tolerance and loyalty. Helga's most prized possessions include a small golden cup.

Hufflepuff is the most inclusive of all the houses: valuing hardwork, dedication, patience, loyalty and fair play rather than a particular aptitute in its members. Hufflepuffs are generally modest about their accomplishments and are cheerful and friendly.

However it should never be understood that Hufflepuffs are dumb and duffers. The famous Mathematician Chota Kujju was from Hufflepuff. Olly is also from Hufflepuff
